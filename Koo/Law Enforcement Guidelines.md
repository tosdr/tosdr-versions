Guidelines for Law Enforcement Agencies
---------------------------------------

![](https://info.kooapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/04/1-vsPXEXChMkKYGNShI3jGDQ-1024x1024-1-300x300.webp)

By Koo App  
April 25, 2022

##### Requests for Information by Indian Law Enforcement Agencies

These guidelines set out Koo’s process for managing requests for information from Indian police, security and other government agencies (Law Enforcement Agencies). These guidelines are in compliance with the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021 (hereinafter “Intermediary Guidelines, 2021”).

In accordance with the Intermediary Guidelines, 2021, Individuals or Private Entities seeking redressal must write to the Koo Resident Grievance Officer at [redressal@kooapp.com](mailto:redressal@kooapp.com)  
  
We reserve the right to withdraw or suspend or terminate Services at any time without prior notice. Grievance Redressal Services or responses to requests will be delayed during such withdrawal or suspension or termination.

##### Legal Process Requirements in India

Any request for information shall only be made in accordance with our [Terms of Service](https://terms-of-service/) and only to the extent provided in Intermediary Guidelines, 2021.

Such requests will be acknowledged by the Nodal Officer within 24 hours of receipt and any information in the possession of Koo will be furnished within 72 hours.

Any blocking orders received by a court order or notification from the Government or its authorized agencies will be resolved within 36 hours.

Indian Law Enforcement Authorities are requested to send requests for information in any of the following manners –

##### Email

* All Indian Law Enforcement Agencies are requested to send requests for information to [nodal.officer@kooapp.com](mailto:nodal.officer@kooapp.com). Usage of any other email address may lead to delays in response to the request.
* Requests must be sent from a Government of India issued email ID and from a Government of India domain name _i.e._, gov.in/.nic.in/<state>.gov.in. If an email is received from a different email id, Koo reserves the right to authenticate the source of the request, which may lead to a delay in responses.

##### Form

All Indian Law Enforcement Agencies may also send their requests by filling this [Form](https://forms.zohopublic.in/bombinatetechnologiespvtltd/form/RequestsfromLawEnforcementAuthorities/formperma/WkO6CPiALpEATxyU91eOFpNG-gHFK9Dyx57-qMGUo0Q).

##### Emergency Requests

In accordance with Intermediary Guidelines, 2021 there will be immediate compliance or compliance no later than 24 hours of blocking orders relating to accounts that are:

* posting content that exposes the private area of an individual
* shows an individual in full or partial nudity
* or shows or depicts an individual in any sexual act or conduct
* or is in the nature of impersonation in an electronic form, including artificially morphed images; or
* child abuse
