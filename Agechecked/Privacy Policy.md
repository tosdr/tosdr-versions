**INFORMATION COLLECTION USE AND STORAGE**

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with Your Information in an iframe/web portal that is hosted securely by us. This information can be used to check your suitability to access age-restricted products or services (“Age Checking”) and send your account information. We do not share Your Information with third parties for any purpose other than Age Checking. We do not share Your Information with the organisation that has asked us to provide this Age Checking Service for them (“Website Operator”).

A Website Operator may send us Your Information and ask us to Age Check you using that information. We may return the results of the Age Check to the Website Operator, but we will not send Your Information to the Website Operator.  
For clarity, a Website Operator will only receive data from us so that they may ascertain your suitability to access the age-restricted service, and not Your Information itself.

Information that we may collect on you, and use to ascertain your suitability to access the age-restricted service could be:

* Your name
    
* Your date of birth or age
    
* Your telephone number
    
* Your address
    
* Your email address
    
* Your IP address
    
* Your payment card information
    
* Information derived from Government issued documentation
    
* Politically exposed persons (PEPs), sanctions and exclusions data
    

Once an age check has taken place any of Your Information that may have been gathered during the age checking process is removed from our system, apart from:

* Your date of birth or age
    
* Politically exposed persons (PEPs), sanctions and exclusions data (if applicable).
    

Thus, the information that remains is anonymised and used to ascertain your suitability to access age-restricted services.
