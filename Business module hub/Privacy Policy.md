Privacy Policy
==============

**Our Commitment to Privacy**

At Business Module Hub your privacy is very important to us. We know you hate SPAM and so do we. That’s why we’ll never sell or share your information with anyone. We respect your rights and will do everything in our power to protect your information. In the interest of full disclosure we provide this notice explaining our online information collection practices. This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Internet Marketing Ninja and applies solely to information collected by this web site.

##### **Information Collection, Use, and Sharing**

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We only have access to information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone. We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship an order.

##### **Contact Form**

In order for us to contact you, a user must complete the contact form first. When completing the form, a user is required to give certain information (such as name and email address). This information is used to contact you about the products/services on our site in which you have expressed interest. At your option, you may also provide demographic information (such as gender or age) about yourself, but it is not required.

##### **Links**

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

##### **Email Policy**

* Business Module Hub has a strict NO-SPAM policy.
* Business Module Hub will never sell or trade your email address to any other.
* Business Module Hub sends commercial email messages to those users who have a pre-existing business relationship with us. A pre-existing business relationship can be referred as, that an user has made purchase from us or has participated in any of our events, polling, surveys etc. Although at any time user may opt out from availing the free email services by following simple steps.

**Business Module Hub Gathers User Information In The Following Processes:**

**OPTIONAL VOLUNTARY INFORMATION** 

We offer the following free services, which require some type of voluntary submission of personal information by users:

1. Electronic newsletters (Dispatches)

We do offer electronic newsletter to our website users. Business Module Hub gathers the email addresses of users who voluntarily subscribe. At any point of time, an user can unsubscribe him/herself by clicking on the link that will be on the bottom of every newsletter. We value your interest.

2. Polling

There may be situations that we (Business Module Hub) may organize a polling session for our beloved users. The poll can be happened about any topic which is booming or topic which attracts various users to interact among them and share the valuable opinion with others.

3. Surveys

Business Module Hub may conduct surveys occasionally to better target the content to our audience. With us a user need not to worry about his her privacy, as we will not share the information about the individuals with any third party.

**COOKIES**

We may place a text file “cookie” in the browser files of your computer system. The cookie itself does not contain any kind of Personal Information although it enables us to relate your use of the website [businessmodulehub.com](https://www.businessmodulehub.com/) to information that you have provided knowingly. A cookie is neither able to read data from your system nor read cookie files created by other third party sites. Business Module Hub uses cookies to track the traffic pattern of a user.

You can always refuse cookies by turning them off from your browser setting, so whenever you open our website you will get a warning message about enabling the cookies.

**SECURITY** 

Business Module Hub operates very secure data networks which are protected by the industry standard firewalls. Our password protection system is pretty much secure that except the authorized person no one will be able to decode it.

**YOU’RE CONSENT**

By using the official website of Business Module Hub, you consent to the collection and use of the information by us. If any point of time we decide to change our privacy policy for the betterment of user experience, we will update it on our website so that no information gets hidden from our users.
