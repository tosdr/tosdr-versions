### **Transit On-Demand**

Any personal information collected is authorized under Section 33(c) of the _Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act, RSA 2000, c. F-25_ (**“FOIP Act”**), as amended from time to time and is managed and protected in accordance with the FOIP Act.  The City of Grande Prairie uses a third-party service provider VIA, who collects your personal information to provide transit booking services.  By clicking the VIA link, you are agreeing to their privacy policy below.  If you have any questions about the collection, use and disclosure of your personal information please contact Steve Harvard at Transit Services at 780-357-7524 or [sharvard@cityofgp.com](mailto:sharvard@cityofgp.com).
