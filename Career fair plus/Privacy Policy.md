What data do we collect?
========================

Career Soft LLC collects various types of data depending on how you use our websites and mobile applications.

Our websites and mobile apps can be used without creating an account to view information about events. Some data we collect without you explicitly providing it:

* Anonymous analytics information about how you use our websites and applications which may include unique device identifiers.

If you create an account on our website or mobile apps, you may or may not provide us with  the following information:

* Personal information, including:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Profile picture
* Etc.

* In addition, for candidates, you may or may not provide us with your educational and work background, including:

* Resume
* GPA
* Major or discipline
* School year
* The types of work positions you are seeking
* Your work authorization or visa status
* Etc.

* For recruiters, you may or may not provide us with:

* Who you are employed by
* Any candidates you may have spoken with
* Ratings or notes that you recorded for any candidate

How do we collect your data?
============================

You directly provide Career Soft LLC with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

* Create a student account through our website or mobile applications
* Are invited and accept an invitation as a recruiter in our system
* Sign up for appointments with recruiters through our system
* Rate or make notes on candidates through our system

Career Soft LLC may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

* Your college, university, or other event-hosting organization may provide us with your email address in order to invite you to the system

How will we use your data?
==========================

Career Soft LLC collects your data so that we can:

* Connect students and candidates with recruiters representing organizations they may be interested in
* Provide recruiters with a way to view, manage, and contact students and candidates they are interested in hiring
* Allow event organizers, including college and university career center staff, to view your data in order to:

* Provide assistance to recruiters and candidates
* Reach out to provide information about the event, promote the event, and for other event-related communications

* Provide customer support
* If you have signed up to receive such communications, Career Soft LLC may send emails to users alerting them of updates to our blog or potential new products or services

What are your data protection rights?
=====================================

Career Soft LLC would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights.

Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to request copies of your personal data held by Career Soft LLC. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Career Soft LLC correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Career Soft LLC to complete any information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Career Soft LLC erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Career Soft LLC restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to Career Soft LLC’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Career Soft LLC transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us:

What are cookies?
=================

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit [https://www.allaboutcookies.org/](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://www.allaboutcookies.org/&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1620334887493000&usg=AOvVaw2VGTN8pWJOhi9VErypYrYy).

How do we use cookies?
======================

Our company uses cookies in a range of ways to improve the experience on our website, including:

* Keeping you signed in
* Understanding how you use our website
* Saving information you enter into our website so that it is not lost between sessions
* To better assist you when you have questions/chats with our customer support staff

How to manage your cookies
==========================

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website (see What are cookies?) tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.
