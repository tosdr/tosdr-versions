Policy on the use of cookies
============================

What is a cookie?

Cookies are temporary text files stored on your computer, tablet or smartphone when you visit our website. Thanks to the information contained in these files, you can for example, automatically connect to your user account, and the contents of your basket will be memorised. We also use cookies to understand how you use our site to continually improve our performance. The content of cookies may vary and so we use four variants: analytics, functional, performance and tracking cookies.

* [Working Cookies](#tab-1)
* [Performance cookies](#tab-2)
* [Analytical cookies](#tab-3)
* [Tracking cookies](#tab-4)

Functional cookies let us store your preferences and automatically connect you to our website. They improve your experience and facilitate using our services.

| qtrans\_front\_language | Infomaniak blog | 1 year | Infomaniak | Displaying the blog in the same language as your last visit. |
| PHPSESSID | Site Infomaniak<br><br>Infomaniak blog | Duration of your visit | Infomaniak | Save users' sessions to facilitate their connections and memorise their preferences. |
| SASESSION, hosting\_session | Site Infomaniak<br><br>Manager | Duration of your visit | Infomaniak | Save users' sessions to facilitate their connections and memorise their preferences. |

| wks\_flavour | Connection page | 4 years | Infomaniak | Checking the internal routing. |

Performance cookies and conversion pixels measure the performance of our advertisements. They also enable us to calculate the exact commission for the partners who promote our services.

| Cookie name | Site | Maximum period of conservation | Place by | Objective |

At Infomaniak, our core mission is to improve our site and our services based on your preferences and your needs. Using analytic cookies, we collect information on the use of our site see what information you find useful or superfluous.
