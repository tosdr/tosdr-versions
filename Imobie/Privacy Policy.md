Your personal information including full name, email address, country, language, mailing address, phone number, postal code, credit card number will be required, to complete transactions.

We have different kinds of Products and different Products may collect different data. Generally, we may collect the data below if you use our Products.

(a) Camera permission: Scan the QR code to process the computer information, then connect the device to the computer and mirror.  
(b) Audio recording permission: Process the built-in speaker sound source and mirror to the computer software.  
(c) Location permission: Use UDP scanning of surrounding devices to process computer information. Display the Wifi name to ensure that the user is on the same network as the computer.  
(d) Background service permission: Ensure the stability of the connection when mirroring.

(5) When you submit content to us:

When you submit a ticket to us for customer support, we may collect your information including your name, email address, age group, product name, and the content of messages to offer you better services.
