News Corporation (“Company,” “News Corp,” “we,” “us”) understands and respects that you care about your personal information and how it is used. We respect your privacy, as further described in our full [Privacy Notice](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/). These highlights set out some points about how we treat your personal information.

Please review the full [Privacy Notice](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice) to understand our privacy practices with respect to our corporate site, [www.newscorp.com](http://www.newscorp.com/)

**PRIVACY NOTICE SCOPE**

News Corp owns, operates, or provides access to [www.newscorp.com](http://www.newscorp.com/) site (the “Site”). The News Corp [Privacy Notice](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice) applies to the Site and describes how we treat your personal information when you use the Site. The Privacy Notice describes in detail what information we collect about you, why we collect it, what we do with it and how to contact us about our privacy practices.

[Read More](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/#scope)

**COLLECTION OF INFORMATION**

We collect personal information from a variety of sources, including:

* Information you provide directly to the Site;
* Information about you that our service providers may also have;
* Information about your device and browsing activities on the Site, including through cookies and other similar technologies.

[Read More](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/#information-we-collect-and-obtain)

**USE OF INFORMATION**

Our use of the personal information we collect will depend on the way you interact with the Site.  For example, we may use the information we collect to:

* Provide our services;
* Improve your experience with our Site;
* Communicate with you and respond to your requests or questions;
* And, as we may otherwise notify you at the point of collection or with your consent.

[Read More](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/#HOW-WE-USE-THE-INFORMATION-WE-OBTAIN)

**YOUR CHOICES AND RIGHTS**

As described further in our Privacy Notice, you may have certain choices and rights related to the information we collect about you, including:

* The opportunity to access, update and correct, delete and/or port information that you choose to share with us, subject to applicable law.
* Choices about the collection and use of information about your device and browsing activities through cookies and other similar technologies, as described in our [Cookie Notice](https://newscorp.com/cookie-notice/).

[Read More](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/#YOUR-CHOICES-AND-RIGHTS)

**SHARING OF INFORMATION**

We may share your information in certain situations, including: 

* With our service providers;
* If the Company or the services is merged with or is sold to another company;
* To comply with the law or to protect or defend the rights of the Company or our users.

[Read More](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/#INFORMATION-WE-SHARE)

**ADDITIONAL INFORMATION**

We maintain administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect and secure your information, but no data transmission or storage, or use of the Internet, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

For more information about our privacy practices, please review the full [Privacy Notice](https://newscorp.com/news-corp-privacy-notice/).
