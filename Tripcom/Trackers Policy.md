In addition, you can request:

§ the categories of personal information collected about you in the 12 months preceding you request;

§ the categories of sources for that personal information collected;

§ the business or commercial purposes for collecting that personal information;

§ the categories of third parties with whom we share that personal information;

§ a copy of the specific pieces of personal information collected; and

§ a list of the personal information we disclosed for business purpose, by category of personal information and by category of recipient, identifying the personal information categories that each recipient category obtained in the previous 12 months.

The rights listed above may not apply in all cases. For example, if you ask us to delete information which we are required by law to keep or have compelling legitimate interests in keeping, we may not be able to delete all of your personal information, in which case we will inform you of the relevant exceptions.

To address any privacy or security questions or concerns, or to make any of these requests, please contact us with details about your request at sg\_dataprotection@trip.com; or

We will respond to your above requests without undue delay. Trip.com shall take reasonable steps to verify customer identity before taking further action on your request:

o    Where you have a Trip.com account, we will ask you to:

1.    Log out of your account and log back in within 24 hours.

2.    Where you have placed an order, provide us with 2 pieces of information of your recent order(s) (i.e. departure date or city, check-in date or hotel name, guest name).

3.    Provide a scanned copy of your passport or government issued ID.

1.    Provide us with 2 pieces of information of your recent order(s) (i.e. departure date or city, check-in date or hotel name, guest name).

2.    Provide a scanned copy of your passport or government issued ID.

Where an authorised agent is making a request on your behalf, we will ask for proof of their authority, such as a copy of their power of attorney.

Trip.com will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your rights under this section.

**Cookie Statement**
====================

We use cookies to deliver our services and to help us improve our website.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that we store on your computer when you use our website. To learn more about cookies, please go to [www.aboutcookies.org](http://www.aboutcookies.org/).

What cookies does Trip.com use?

We use the following cookies:

**Essential cookies**

·   These are cookies that our website cannot operate without. They include, for e.g., cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website, use a shopping cart and pay for travel products.

**Non-essential cookies**

·   **Functionality cookies:** These are used to recognise you when you return to our website. This enables us to personalise our content for you and remember your preferences (for e.g., your choice of language, region and currency).

·   **Analytics cookies:** These allow us to count the number of visitors to our website and to see how they move around it. This helps us improve the way our website works, for e.g., by ensuring that users are finding what they are looking for easily.

·   **Targeting cookies:** These cookies record your visit to our website, the pages you have visited and the internal links you have followed. We will use this information to make our website and the travel products we display to you more relevant to your interests.

·   **Advertising cookies**: These allow us to personalise the adverts that we show you and that you see across the internet.  To opt-out of these cookies, please go to [https://us.trip.com/pages/treatyforcookie](https://us.trip.com/pages/treatyforcookie).

We use session and persistent cookies. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your computer and are activated the next time you visit our website.
