Cookie and similar technologies
-------------------------------

A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser directories. Cookies are created when you visit a website. When you visit a website again cookies are retrieved. Cookies are used to store user settings, login information and other purposes. Some other technologies may also be referred to as cookies if used in a similar manner.

Below are the categories of cookies used by our services:

| Category | Explanation |
| --- | --- |
| Necessary | This category of cookies are used to provide core website functionality, load balancing, access to secure areas and to combat fraud and abuse. They may also be used for ad frequency capping and aggregated ad reporting. The website or ads cannot properly function without these cookies. |
| Preferences | These cookies are used to remember information that changes the way the site behaves or looks, such as your preferred language, your translation suggestions, the region you are in, font, text size, theme, etc. |
| Statistics | These types of cookies are used for reporting and collection of anonymous site usage statistics. |
| Marketing | These types of cookies are used to track users across multiple websites with the intention of displaying more relevant and engaging advertisement. When these cookies are disabled users will see non-personalized ads. |

You may choose to configure your web browser to refuse cookies. If they do so, note that some parts of our Services may not function properly. You may select the categories of cookies that you allow our Serives to set in [cookie settings](javascript: renderCookieConsentDialog(true); void(0);). If you previously given cookie consent you may [withdraw consent](javascript: withdrawCookieConsent(); void(0);).
