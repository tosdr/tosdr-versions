* With all international shipments there is a possibility of delay. We do not have any control or influence over customs clearance and other regulatory screening.

* All international orders require an accurate address and must include a telephone number in order to expedite shipping and prevent lost shipments.
    
* A lost shipment must be reported within 10 business days of delivery in order for a claim to be filed, otherwise your request will be denied.
