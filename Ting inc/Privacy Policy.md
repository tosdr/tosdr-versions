Privacy policy
==============

Collection and Use of Personal Information
------------------------------------------

While you’re using this site, we collect the following information about you:

* Usage activity about how you interact with it, such as what content you viewed and which areas you visited;
    
* Information about your computer such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and operating system;
    
* Anything you put into a form on this site.
    

In order to provide you with Internet and service for that Internet, we also collect the following personal information from you when you’re our customer or in other situations like when you’re still just considering if you’re going to be our customer:

* Contact information such as your name, email address, mailing address, service address, and phone number;
    
* Billing Information such as credit card number and billing address;
    
* Other unique Identifiers such as your user name, account number, and a hash of your password (this means we can’t see your actual password).
    

We use this information to:

* See how well we're doing with website design;
    
* Fulfill your order;
    
* Send you an order confirmation;
    
* Respond to customer service requests;
    
* Administer your account, including allowing you to (a) login to, navigate, and use our website; (b) view and adjust the services you have purchased from us inside your account; and (c) pay your bill or update your billing information;
    
* Send you communications about how to use our services;
    
* Send you communications about new service offerings;
    
* Solicit your feedback on the quality of our customer service; and
    
* Respond to your questions and concerns.  
      
    Be aware, this Privacy Statement does not apply to any third-party application or equipment providers: this means any equipment or app you may use with Ting Internet services but that are not controlled by us. If you set up an account with or receive services from one of these third parties, you will be subject to that third party’s privacy policy, terms and conditions, and acceptable use policy.

Tracking technologies
---------------------

We use technologies such as cookies to gather information about how you and others interact with our website. For example, we will know how many people access specific areas or features within our site and which links they clicked on. We use this aggregated information to understand and optimize how this site is used and to provide content and features that are of use and interest to you.

Security
--------

The security of your personal information is important to us. When you provide sensitive information (such as a credit card number) to us, we encrypt the transmission of that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). We also follow generally-accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. Each of us gets regular training about how to handle sensitive data and how important it is.

If you have any questions about security, you can contact us at [help@ting.com](mailto:help@ting.com).

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services, fulfill our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information to provide you services, please contact us at [help@ting.com](mailto:help@ting.com).
