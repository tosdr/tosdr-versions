Privacy Policy
==============

InviZible Pro does not collect or share any personal or sensitive user data.

InviZible Pro forwards internet traffic to Tor, DNSCrypt and I2P networks. For detailed information, please visit the official pages of the mentioned projects.

InviZible Pro is fully open-source. Please visit [https://github.com/Gedsh/InviZible](https://github.com/Gedsh/InviZible) for source code.

  
Tor site: [https://www.torproject.org](https://www.torproject.org/)

DNSCrypt site: [https://dnscrypt.info](https://dnscrypt.info/)

Purple I2P site: [https://i2pd.website](https://i2pd.website/)
