Bensound: Privacy Policy
========================

Beware of scammers impersonating Bensound with fake job offers! You’ll find all our legitimate job offers on bensound.com/careers.  
Please report suspicious activity to support@bensound.com

COOKIES
=======

Cookies are small files saved on your computer to store data about your connections.  
This site uses cookies to facilitate the order process and display language and currency.

The site also uses Google Analytics analysis tool that collects information about your browsing with cookies. These data are collected for the purposes of statistical analysis and are confidential and anonymous.

DISABLE COOKIES
===============

You can disable cookies by changing the preferences in your browser, but this may prevent the normal functioning of the site.

To block or enable cookies:  
\- Internet Explorer: [http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookies](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-vista/block-or-allow-cookies)  
\- Firefox: [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)

DATA GATHERED WHEN CREATING AN ACCOUNT AND ORDER
================================================

When you create an account on this site, we store the information needed for orders in a client file. These data are confidential and will not be disclosed to third parties, with the sole exception of YouTube channel ID/URLs.  
In order to whitelist YouTube channels, we have to gather and store your YouTube channel ID/URL and share it with our content ID licensing partner Orfium (www.orfium.com), who facilitates our content ID licensing.  
You can at any time request to view, edit or delete your client informations by sending us your request by mail or email with a copy of your ID.
