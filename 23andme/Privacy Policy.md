To keep things simple, we use the same terms here as in our [Terms of Service](https://www.23andme.com/en-int/legal/terms-of-service/). We’ll let you know in this Privacy Statement if we have a new or different definition for a term. You should read our entire Privacy Statement, but if you only have a few minutes you can take a look at this [privacy summary](https://www.23andme.com/en-int/privacy/).
