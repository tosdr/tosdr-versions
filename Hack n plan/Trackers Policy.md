**2\. DEFINITION OF COOKIES**

A cookie is a small text file that is stored for a fixed period of time on the hard drive of your device at the time you access certain websites (hereinafter the “Cookies” in plural, and “Cookie” in singular). The Cookies allow websites, among other things, to store and retrieve information about browsing habits and behavior of the user or device from which you connect, to serve custom navigation, and thus enhance the experience when browsing the web.

**3\. TYPE OF COOKIES USED**

We use cookies on our Website, both session and persistent, first-party or third-party cookies. For your information:

* Session cookies are cookies that are automatically deleted when you close your browser.
* Persistent cookies are cookies that remain stored on the user’s device even after you close the browser.
* First-party cookies are those that are sent to the user’s device by the web in which you browse.
* Third-party cookies are those that are sent to the user’s device by a website that is not managed by the web where you browse, but by another entity.

Specifically, we use the following types of cookies:

* Analytical cookies: those that, well treated by us or by third parties, allows us to evaluate the behavior of users of our Website, and thus help us improve our services, performance, and user experience.
* Customization cookies: those that allow users to access the service with some general features predefined in the terminal or that the user defines. For example, the language, the type of browser you access the service with, the design of the content selected, and the geolocation of the terminal or place you access the service from, amongst others.
* Technical cookies: those that allow the user to navigate through the Website or application and use the different options or services provided. For example, control traffic and data communication, identify the session, access restricted web sections, remember the elements of an order, make an application for registration or participation in an event, use security features for the navigation, and store content for broadcasting video or sound.

Cookies are used exclusively for the purposes stated in this Cookie Policy.

While cookies are not required for browsing the Website if you use them you will enjoy a better experience. You can change the settings of your browser to erase or block, but if you do it is possible that some services or functionality may not work properly.
