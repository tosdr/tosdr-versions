### Fairness and Transparency

We are committed to the ethical use of personal data

When you trust us with your data, you help us advance treatments to some of the world’s most difficult health problems and ensure fair and equitable access to those treatments when they become available. We honor that trust by telling you how and why we collect, use and share your data. We also make it easy for you to tell us when you want to change your privacy choices.
