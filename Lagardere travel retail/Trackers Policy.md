Cookie Policy
=============

Pursuant to Article 13 of EU Regulation 679/2016 on the protection of personal data (hereafter the “Regulation”), the company Lagardère Travel Retail SAS (hereafter the “Company”), is the Data Controller of the website www.lagardere-tr.com (hereafter the “Site”).

When visiting our Site, cookies may be placed on the browsers of your computer, tablet, smartphone or any other device.

**Cookies, what is it?**

A “cookie” is a limited size file, generally made up of letters and numbers, sent by the Internet server to the browser cookie file located on your computer hard drive when navigation within the Site.

Cookies have a maximum validity of 13 months. At the end of this time, they are deleted from your device.

**Cookies, who are they?**

Several types of cookies, with different purposes, are used on the Site. Some are necessary to allow you using all our services.

**Strictly necessary cookies**

Necessary cookies for the functioning of our Site are cookies without which you may not be able to use all the services offered. These cookies are deposited by the Company and only concern the functioning.

Strictly necessary cookies that we emit on our Site allow the visitor to access the Site systematically through the same hosting server.

**Analytics cookies**

These cookies allow us to know the use and performances of our Site, and thus to improve its functioning. These cookies can be deposited by the Company or by third parties’ providers.

Analytics cookies we emit on our Site allow us to:

* Count the number of visitors,
* Establish statistics about traffic and use
* Analyze the navigation in order to adapt the Site,
* And ensure its coherence.

Our Site uses the _MAtomo_ traffic measurement solution (see “Third party cookies” section). To refuse cookies deposit by the _MATOMO_ traffic measurement solution, please read the terms set below.

**Cookies, what are your choices?**

Some cookies are stored on your device without you being able to refuse them. These include cookies whose sole purpose is to enable or to facilitate electronic communication (e.g., “session identifiers” cookies).

You have several options to manage cookies. Any setting you may undertake may change your navigation within the Site and your access conditions to specific services requiring cookies use.

Cookies installation is not mandatory, and you can configure your browser to refuse installation. Your browser can also be set to notify cookies deposited on your device and to ask you to accept or refuse them. You can accept or refuse cookies on a case by case basis or refuse them systematically.

Your choices are not final, and you can change them at any time.

If you accept cookies recording on your computer, cookies embedded in the pages and contents you consult may be temporarily stored in a dedicated area of your device. They shall only be readable by the issuer.

You can refuse cookies recording at any time. In this case, you will not be able to benefit from a certain number of functionalities, in particular those which need to identify you.

Where appropriate, we decline any liability for the consequences of our services poor functioning resulting from the impossibility for us to record or consult the cookies necessary for their operation and that you have refused or deleted.

**How to exercise your choices according to the browser you use?**

For your choices’ management concerning cookies, the configuration of each browser is different. This configuration is described in the help menu of your browser.

**For Microsoft Edge**

To change your level of tracking prevention, clear your browsing data, help improve Microsoft Edge, and more, select Settings and more > Settings > Privacy, search, and services.

For more information, see the following web page: **[Microsoft Edge, browsing data, and privacy - Microsoft Support](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/microsoft-edge-browsing-data-and-privacy-bb8174ba-9d73-dcf2-9b4a-c582b4e640dd)**

**For Safari**

In your browser, choose Edit menu > Preferences. Click on Security. Click on Show cookies. Select the cookies of your choice and click on Delete or Delete all. After deleting the cookies, click Done.

Fore more information, see the following web page: [**https://support.apple.com/fr-fr/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac**](https://support.apple.com/fr-fr/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac)

**For Chrome**

Click the Tools menu icon. Select Options. Click the Advanced Options tab and navigate to the “Privacy” section. Click the Show cookies button. Select the files of your choice and delete them. Click “Close” to return to your browser.

For more information, see the following web page: [**http://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=fr&hlrm=en&answer=95647**](http://support.google.com/chrome/bin/answer.py?hl=fr&hlrm=en&answer=95647)

**For Firefox**

Go to the “Tools” tab of the browser and select the “Options” menu. In the window that appears, choose “Privacy” and click “Show cookies”. Select the files of your choice and delete them.

For more information, see the following web page: [**http://support.mozilla.org/fr/kb/Activer%20et%20d%C3%A9sactiver%20les%20cookies**](http://support.mozilla.org/fr/kb/Activer%20et%20d%C3%A9sactiver%20les%20cookies)
