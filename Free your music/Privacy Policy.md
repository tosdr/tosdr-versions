**Cookie policy**

We use cookies to collect information and learn your internet behaviour pattern and preferences. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some of the finest features of our Platform.

Cookies are small data files stored on the hard drive of your computer or mobile device by a website. You need to know that there are two kinds of cookies:

1. session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and
2. persistent cookies (which stay on your computer or mobile device until you delete it).

We collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site, which is not linked to any information that allows to identify you. The information is used to track visitor’s movement on the Site, and to gather visitor’s demographic information.

**General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**

We would like to ensure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights in accordance with the GDPR laws. Therefore, you have the following rights:

1. _the right to access your personal data_: You can request and you are entitled to obtain any of your personal data we process, where it comes from and what we use it for. You can obtain information about how long we store your data and who receives data about you. Your right to access may, however, be restricted by legislation, protection of other persons’ privacy, and considerations for our business and practices.
2. _the right to rectification and ensure of data_: If you have discovered that the personal information we have about you is incorrect, incomplete or irrelevant, you are entitled to have the data corrected or erased subject to the restrictions that follow from existing legislation and rights to process data.
3. _the right to the restriction of use_: If you believe that the data we have processed is incorrect or incomplete, or if you have objected to the use of the data, you have the right to demand the imposition of restrictions as far as the processing of your personal data is concerned.
4. _the right to data portability_: If you ask us to do so, we will pass on your personal information in a structured, usable format.
5. _the right to object to processing_: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data if we process it for direct marketing purposes.
6. _the right to make a decision about automated individual profiling_: You also shall have the right to object to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling. In these situations, FreeyYourMusic will guarantee suitable measures to safeguard your rights, mainly the right to human intervention when reviewing such cases. You will be notified if any automated decision-making is performed in relation to your personal data.
7. _the right to withdrawal of consent_: If we process personal data based on your consent, you can withdraw your consent to disclose data at any given time. Please also note that we will continue to use your personal data if we have to fulfil a legal or contractual obligation. 
8. _the right to be forgotten_: The right to obtain from us the erasure of all your personal data given to us under the Policy without undue delay. If you will make such a request, we shall have the obligation to erase all your collected and processed personal data without undue delay.
