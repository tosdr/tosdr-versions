In addition to that listed in the Privacy Statement, the types of personal information we may collect include, but are not limited to:

* Your opensource.com username
* Your opensource.com password
* Other information that Red Hat collects online from you and maintains in association with your opensource.com account, such as:
    * Any website address you provide

If necessary, you may also contact us at the address in the Privacy Statement and describe the changes you want made to the information you have previously provided.
