Important Notice Regarding Health Services
==========================================

Pacer may utilize connections to the Apple Health App (“HealthKit”), Google Fit, and other third-party fitness, health, and tracking applications and services (“Health Services”). You can choose to connect and share information with Health Services. If you grant access for us to read data from Health Services, we can read information such as your daily activities and related information like activity recordings, activity steps, activity distance, activity durations, calories burned, blood pressure, heart rate, date of birth, height, weight, gender, and flights climbed, amongst other data, from Health Services. Likewise, if you grant access for us to write data to Health Services, we can add information such as your daily activities and related information like activity recordings, activity steps, activity distance, activity durations, calories burned, blood pressure, heart rate, height, weight, and flights climbed, amongst other data, to Health Services.

Pacer cannot read data from or write data to Health Services unless you grant access. You can remove access at any time inside the Health Services.

Pacer only uses your Health Services data to provide and improve our Pacer’s functionalities. We don’t use your Health Services data for any marketing or advertising purposes, or share with or sell to advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers.

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Privacy Policy, Pacer’s use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Pacer is in no way responsible for the protection of any of your information that you agree to store with Health Services, which is governed by the privacy policies and other terms of the applicable Health Service. You and the Health Service are solely responsible for the protection of such information. Please review the Health Service's applicable policies and procedures before granting permission to sync your health data and/or other information with the Health Service.

What Information Do We Collect
==============================

Pacer collects information about you, including information that directly or indirectly identifies you if you choose to share it with Pacer. We receive information when you choose to use our services. We also collect information about how you use our Services. You also have other opportunities to share information with us.

For example:

Account, Profile and Contact Information
----------------------------------------

We collect basic account information such as your display name, year of birth, birthdate in some cases, email, and password to secure your account and provide you with access to the Services.

We collect other profile information such as your profile picture, profile description and your region or country.

If you purchase a physical item from us that requires fulfillment, we will also collect fulfillment information such as your name, phone number, billing address, and shipping address so that we can fulfill your order and contact you about fulfillment issues.

We will also use your email or other contact information that you provide to us if you contact us about support issues or sales inquiries.

Fitness and Wellness Data
-------------------------

Fitness and wellness data includes data that you provide to us about your fitness habits, fitness and wellness goals, body and vitals measurements like body weight, BMI, height, gender, blood pressure, heart rate, step length and exercise activities.

It also includes data that is imported or ingested from your connected devices’ sensors when using the Services including step counts, accelerometer readings, barometer readings, and location sensor readings that include GPS coordinates, timestamps, elevation, speed and bearing.

We collect this data in order to provide the Services to you including reporting and analytics, fitness and nutrition recommendations, participation in online challenges and races, and product and feature recommendations.

Data from Devices and Third-Party Accounts & Services
-----------------------------------------------------

We also collect Personal Data, including Fitness and Wellness Data when you connect a device or service that integrates with the Services, such as other fitness tracking devices or apps. We may also collect certain information about the device or product such as make, model, and identifying number provided by the device manufacturer.

If you access the Services with Third-Party Account Logins such as Facebook, Apple or Google, we will collect information that you have agreed to make available such as your name and email address. This information is provided to Pacer under the privacy policies of the Third-Party Account. You can control the information that Pacer receives by adjusting the privacy controls in your Third-Party Account.

Pacer only uses your Health Services (Apple Health App (“HealthKit”), Google Fit, and other third-party fitness, health, and tracking applications and services) data to provide and improve our Pacer’s functionalities. We don’t use your Health Services data for any marketing or advertising purposes, or share with or sell to advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers.

Location Data
-------------

We collect and process location information when you sign up for and use the Services. By default, we do not track your location while you are not using Pacer, but in order to use some of the Services it is necessary for us to track your device location while you are using the Services.

If you would like to stop device location tracking, you may do so at any time by adjusting your device settings.

Content that You Share on Our Platform
--------------------------------------

We gather information from the photos, posts, comments, likes, ratings, routes, reviews, and other content you share on the Services.

Contacts and Friends Lists
--------------------------

You may choose to give Pacer access to your mobile device’s contacts information or Facebook friends list for the purpose of finding your contacts on Pacer. We collect this information only with your explicit consent, and you may remove that consent at any time from your device privacy settings or Facebook settings. We do not retain this information on our servers.

Payment Information
-------------------

When you make a payment to Pacer, you may provide payment information such as your payment card or other payment details. We use Payment Card Industry compliant third-party payment services and we do not store your credit card information.

Technical Information and Logs
------------------------------

When you use our Services we collect technical information from your browser, computer, or mobile device. This technical information includes device information, cookies, log files and analytics information.

The Services use logs to protect and secure the Services and analyze aggregate usage trends for the purpose of product improvement.

The information stored in these logs includes IP addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type and version, timestamps with time zones, device screen size, system settings like preferred language/locale, technical performance metrics such as network, energy and memory usage, details about technical exceptions and crashes, and product usage data such as time spent on pages and button clicks or taps. Logs can be linked to session IDs, Pacer account IDs and device identifiers.

Other Information
-----------------

We may collect information from you through third-parties, such as when we collect your feedback through surveys or market research.

We use the information we collect and receive to provide the Services. We use the information we collect about you, your followers, and your activities to make recommendations to you while you are using our services. For example, we may recommend workout programs, other Pacer members to follow, routes to explore, groups to join, or new challenges to enter.

-----------------------

We use the information we collect to improve the Services. This includes a thorough analysis of product usage and technical logs.

--------------------------------

We use the information we collect to provide support in response to your customer support requests or sales inquiries.

We may also, with your consent, send you push notifications or emails that are promotional in nature. You must opt in to join our email list, and you can opt out at any time by clicking or tapping the unsubscribe button at the bottom of each email communication.

To communicate with you by push notification
--------------------------------------------

We use the information we collect to send push notifications to your registered devices if you provide permission to do so. Notifications may be promotional in nature. ----------------------------

We use the information we collect to process your subscription.

-------------------------------------------------
