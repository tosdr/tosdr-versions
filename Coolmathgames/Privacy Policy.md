The Services allow users to play interactive games. We automatically collect information about your use of the Services, such as games played, scores, where you left off in the game, the length of time that you use our Services, and other activities on the Website.

We also automatically collect or analyze information about your browser and/or device. This information may include: IP address; device identifiers; device name and model; operating system type, name and version.

We may combine this information with other information that we have collected about you. Please see the section “Analytics and Tracking” below for more information.
