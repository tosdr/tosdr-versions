**Cookies**  
Google Analytics informs us about how you use the Lingerie Lowdown® website
=========================================================================================

Lingerie Lowdown® puts small files (known as ‘cookies’) onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the site.

Cookies are used to:

* Measure how you use our website so it can be updated and improved based on your needs
* Remember the notifications you’ve seen so that we don’t show them to you again
* Lingerielowdown.com cookies aren’t used to identify you personally
* You’ll normally see a message on the site before we store a cookie on your computer

Find out more about **[how to manage cookies](http://www.aboutcookies.org/)**.

### How cookies are used on lingerielowdown.com

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use lingerielowdown.com. We do this to help make sure the site is meeting the needs of our users and to help us make improvements, eg improving site search.

Google Analytics stores information about:

* The pages you visit on lingerielowdown.com
* How long you spend on each lingerielowdown.com page
* How you got to the site
* What you click on while you’re visiting the site

The cookies don’t collect or store your personal information (eg your name or address) so this information can’t be used to identify who you are when you are visiting the website.

We don’t allow Google to use or share our analytics data and we don’t share this data with any third parties.
