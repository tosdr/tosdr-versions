### Log Files

Canyouseeme.org follows a standard procedure of using log files. The information collected by log files include -

* IP(Internet Protocol) Address
* Web Browser type
* Date and time stamp
* Referring/exit pages

These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site and gathering demographic information.
