### Do We Use Cookies?

Yes. Cookies are small files that can be transferred to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser. Cookies enable us to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information that you previously shared with us, such as your setting preferences. We use cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits, to keep track of advertisements, to track visitors through our Website, and to compile data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer a better experience in the future. If you prefer, you can choose to have your browser notify you each time a cookie is being sent or to refuse cookies altogether. If you turn your cookies off, some of our services may not function properly.
