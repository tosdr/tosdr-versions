When you contact Us through [info@pixlr.com](mailto:info@pixlr.com), [creators@pixlr.com](mailto:creators@pixlr.com), [education@pixlr.com](mailto:education@pixlr.com) or [legal@pixlr.com](mailto:legal@pixlr.com), you may be required to provide Us your Personal Data such as name, email address and, at your own discretion, other information you decide to share with Us, that We may collect from you.
