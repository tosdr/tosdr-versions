Redemption of Vouchers Online:

To use the OTOcoupon in full or part payment for orders made Online:

* When proceeding to checkout, select ‘’OTOcoupon”.
    
* Enter the 20-digit Voucher number.
    
* Select the ‘Confirm’ button to proceed with the payment.
    
* If the value of the items purchased Online is less than the total value of the Voucher, you can re-enter the same Voucher details on another occasion to redeem the remaining balance on your Voucher, also in combination with additional (up to three) OTOcoupon.
    
* If the value of the items purchased online is more than the total value of the Voucher you will need to pay the balance using additional Vouchers, simply by clicking on the “+” button which then opens another field to enter an additional Voucher PIN.
