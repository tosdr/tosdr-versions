* **Device information.** We collect device specific information such as IP address, access dates and times, hardware and software information, device information and cookie data.
* **Geo-location Information.** When you use certain features of the Website, we may collect information about your approximate location as determined through data such as your IP address.

POLICY FOR ADDRESSING THE DISSEMINATION OF TERRORIST CONTENT
============================================================

_Last Updated: Aug 02, 2022_

We, **Media Enterprises TV, s.r.o.**, having its registered office at Krakovská 1366/25, Nové Město, 110 00 Prague 1, Czech Republic, Business registration No. (IČO): 140 01 381, registered in the Commercial Register administered by the Municipal Court in Prague, Section C, Inset 189029 (hereinafter referred to as the “**Provider**” or „**we**“), the provider of the website [www.sheer.com](https://www.sheer.com/home) (hereinafter referred to as the “**Website**”) are committed to actively participate in addressing the dissemination of terrorist content online. Any content intended to praise, promote, or aid terrorist or any other violent criminal organizations is strictly prohibited on the Website. These organizations are not allowed to use the Website for any purpose, including recruitment.

### 1\. What is Terrorist Content?

In particular, we consider terrorist content to be any content that:

(I) incites the commission one of the following terrorist offences:

* attacks upon a person’s life which may cause death;
* attacks upon the physical integrity of a person;
* kidnapping or hostage-taking;
* causing extensive destruction to a government or public facility, a transport system, an infrastructure facility, including an information system, a fixed platform located on the continental shelf, a public place or private property likely to endanger human life or result in major economic loss;
* seizure of aircraft, ships or other means of public or goods transport;
* manufacture, possession, acquisition, transport, supply or use of explosives or weapons, including chemical, biological, radiological or nuclear weapons, as well as research into, and development of, chemical, biological, radiological or nuclear weapons;
* release of dangerous substances, or causing fires, floods or explosions, the effect of which is to endanger human life;
* interfering with or disrupting the supply of water, power or any other fundamental natural resource, the effect of which is to endanger human life;
* illegal system interference and/or data interference, where a significant number of information systems have been affected and/or a serious damage has been caused and/or were committed against a critical infrastructure information system;
* threatening to commit any of the above listed acts;

(hereinafter collectively referred to as the “**Terrorist Offences**”) where such content, directly or indirectly, such as by the glorification of terrorist acts, advocates the commission of Terrorist Offences, thereby causing a danger that one or more Terrorist Offences may be committed;

(II) solicits a person or a group of persons to commit or contribute to the commission of one of the Terrorist Offences;

(III) solicits a person or a group of persons to participate in the activities of a terrorist group;

(IV) provides instruction on the making or use of explosives, firearms or other weapons or noxious or hazardous substances, or on other specific methods or techniques for the purpose of committing or contributing to the commission of one of the Terrorist Offences;

(V) constitutes a threat to commit one of the Terrorist Offences;

(VI) depicts the insignia, logos, or symbols of violent criminal or terrorist organizations in order to praise or promote them.

This anti-terrorist content policy applies to photos, videos, video descriptions, comments, live streams, chat messages and any other content or feature of the Website. Keep in mind that this isn't a complete list. Please note that this policy also applies to external links in your content. This can include clickable URLs, verbally directing users to other sites, as well as other forms.

### 2\. How to Address Terrorist Content?

If you find content on our Website that violates this policy, please report it using the form at [https://www.sheer.com/support](https://www.sheer.com/support). Any content we suspect might represent terrorist content will be removed and reported to law enforcement in accordance with the applicable laws.

The Provider has established a contact point for the receipt and processing of official removal orders concerning terrorist content issued by the competent state authorities: [https://www.sheer.com/support](https://www.sheer.com/support). This contact point may be addressed in English and Czech language. After receiving the official removal order we will make every possible effort to remove the reported terrorist content or disable access to it as soon as possible and in any event within one hour of receipt of the removal order.

### 3\. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time in accordance with this provision. If we make changes to this policy, we will post the revised policy on the Website and update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy.
