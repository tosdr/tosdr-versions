These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

### Who do we share your data with?

**No One.**

### How long do we retain your data? Who can see your data?

For users that contact us via our website (if any), we store the personal information they provide in our local database till needed unless we’re asked to delete it. Website administrators can see your information as provided in the form you submit.

### What rights do you have over your data?

You can request that we erase any personal data we hold about you any time. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

### Where do we send your data?

**Nowhere,** except in our local offline database (and only what you have shared via the contact form).
