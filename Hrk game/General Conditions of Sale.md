Refund Policy
-------------

You can request a refund for any purchase on HRK and it doesn't matter what is the reason. It's possible that you made the purchase by mistake; you wanted to have a one time experience on HRK Mystery Key and now want to refund your purchase; you may haven't read the product description correctly.

Issue a refund for any reason, if the request is made within twenty-four hours of purchase, and the title(s) on HRK is still unused and not redeemed. There are more details below:

**Where Refunds Apply**

The HRK refund offer, within twenty-four hours of purchase, applies to products that are purchased on HRK. Here is an overview of how refunds work with other types of purchases.

**Refunds on HRK Mystery Key**

HRK offers a one-time refund for games purchased on HRK Mystery Key. The title(s) are refundable twenty-four hours of purchase if they are unused in HRK inventory. To avoid any kind of abuse for our refund policy, we only offer the refund for HRK Mystery Key 1 time for each consumer. Any refund request after the first purchase from HRK Mystery Key will be denied. 

**Refunds on HRK Hot Deals**

Any Hot Deal product purchased on HRK is refundable within twenty-four hours of purchase if they are unused in HRK inventory.

**Refunds on Cryptocurrencies**

If you have paid for a service/product using Cryptocurrencies and ask for a refund later, the amount of the product value in EURO will be refunded for you, not the crypto amount you have sent us.

**Refund on Bundles**

You can receive a full refund for any bundle purchased on HRK, so long as none of the items in the bundle are used. 

**Purchases Made Outside of HRK**

HRK cannot provide refunds for purchases made outside of HRK. (for example, Redeem codes purchased from third parties).

**Refunds on Gifts**

We are unable to offer refunds for gifts after they have been redeemed by the recipient.

**Abuse**

Refunds are designed to remove the risk from purchasing titles on HRK—not as a way to get free games. If it appears to us that you are abusing refunds, we may stop offering them to you. We do not consider it abuse to request a refund on a title that was purchased just before a sale and then immediately rebuying that title for the sale price.

**How to request a refund**

You can request a refund by creating a ticket on HRK Support Center => Refund Department  
You will be issued a full refund of your purchase to your HRK Wallet within forty-eight hours of approval.
