(Updated 14/05/2020)

  

At imagiLabs we want to be transparent about how we collect and use the data related to you.  We use cookies to provide the best experience to everyone visiting imagiLabs website. For more information, please read our Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at support@imagilabs.com 

  

**WHAT IS A COOKIE**

Cookie is a  small text file that is  stored on your computer or mobile device. Cookies may be "first-party cookies", they are set by the website that you are visiting or "third-party cookies"such as cookies that provide analytics or advertising services on the website that you are visiting. 

  

**WHERE WE PLACE THE COOKIES**

* On our websites 
* On sites we host for our users.
* In the emails we send.

  

**THE COOKIES WE USE**

We use the following categories of cookies:

  

**Strictly Necessary Cookies**

The cookies that are required for the operation of imagiLabs website. They include, for example, cookies that enable the use of a shopping cart.

  
  

**Functionality Cookies**

The cookies that help to remember the information of the visitor and settings when the website is  revisited in the future. For example,  language, currency selection, watching a video, commenting on a blog. 

  
  

**Analytical/Performance Cookies**

The cookies that are used to collect information on how the visitors of our website interact with it. We use this information to improve the performance of our website and to personalize the visitor experience. 

  

**Advertising Cookies**

We want to make sure that the advertising displayed on our website is relevant to the visitor, therefore, we use the cookies to display relevant ads and also to understand the performance of the ads displayed in the website. These cookies remember what you have looked at on the website. This information might be shared with other organizations, for example, advertisers.

  
  

**Third-party cookies**

These cookies include the cookies from social media platforms or embedded content from Youtube.We have no direct control over the information that is collected by the third-party cookies. Please refer to the relevant third party's website in order to get more information on how  to disable or reject these cookies.

  
  

**HOW TO MANAGE THE COOKIE SETTINGS**

The law protects website users. You can refuse to accept cookies from our website at any time. In order to do this you would need to  edit the  settings on your browser. Below you can find the links that would provide you more information of how to manage the cookies depending on your browser. 

* [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416?co=GENIE.Platform=Desktop&hl=en)
* [Apple Safari](https://support.apple.com/kb/ph21411?locale=en_US)
* [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
* [Microsoft Internet Explorer](https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/17442/windows-internet-explorer-delete-manage-cookies)
* [Microsoft Edg](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-10-microsoft-edge-and-privacy)
* [Opera](https://www.opera.com/help/tutorials/security/privacy/)

  

Please note, that if cookies are disabled, not all features might operate as intended.
