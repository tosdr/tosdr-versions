Privacy Policy
==============

Last updated: October 8, 2024

Welcome to **YouThumb.net**! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information Collection and Use
------------------------------

**YouThumb.net** does not directly collect any personal information from its visitors. However, we serve advertisements through **Google AdSense**, which may collect data for personalized advertising.

Google AdSense
--------------

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or by visiting [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

Cookies
-------

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. While we do not use cookies directly, our ad partner Google may use cookies to personalize the ads you see.

If you prefer to disable cookies, you can do so through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how some ads are displayed.

External Links
--------------

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those other websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.

Changes to This Policy
