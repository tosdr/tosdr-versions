[Features](https://opentracksapp.com/features)
==============================================

### ![](/static/img/opentracks-logo.svg) _Sharing:_ you decide if, when, with whom, and how to share your data.

### ![](/static/img/opentracks-logo.svg) _Elevation gain:_ estimate your traveled elevation gain

### ![](/static/img/opentracks-logo.svg) _Bluetooth LE sensor:_ collect your data like heart rate

### ![](/static/img/opentracks-logo.svg) _Photos and Markers:_ mark interesting places while tracking

### ![](/static/img/osmdashboard-logo.svg) _Online & offline maps_: take what you need with you

### ![](/static/img/osmdashboard-logo.svg) _Theming_: style the maps how you like it

### ![](/static/img/osmdashboard-logo.svg) _Compass_: shows your direction

### ![](/static/img/osmdashboard-offline-logo.svg) _Pure offline version_: for high privacy requirements there is a pure offline version available which doesnâ€™t have the permission to access the internet. [Privacy](#Privacy)
===================

OpenTracks and OSM Dashboard:

* _do not contain any in-app analytics_
* _do not use Google Play Services_
* _have no cloud integration_
