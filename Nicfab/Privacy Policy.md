Session cookies expire at the end of the session or when the user closes the web browser. ‚Äú_First-party_‚Äù cookies are those set by this site; ‚Äú_Third-party_‚Äù cookies are of a different domain than this one, and they are set up because they use functions managed by third parties. _Session cookies_ (which are not stored permanently on the user‚Äôs computer and disappear when the browser is closed) and are strictly limited to the transmission of session identifiers (consisting of random numbers generated by the server) necessary to allow safe browsing and efficiency of the site.
