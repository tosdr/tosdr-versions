As of January 1, 2020, the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 («CCPA») provides California residents («Consumer(s)») certain rights with respect to their personal information, as this term is defined under the CCPA. * Appoint an agent to exercise their rights under the CCPA, provided that a duly executed notarized power of attorney is presented and provided that the agent has information deemed sufficient to allow us to verify the identity of the Consumer in question and to locate their information in our systems;

Regardless, we respect the California residents’ right to exclude personal information from such sharing arrangements and to thereby opt out of any future transfer of their personal information.

If the CCPA is applicable to you and you would like to record such preference, please e-mail us at [legal@builds.io](mailto:legal@builds.io).
