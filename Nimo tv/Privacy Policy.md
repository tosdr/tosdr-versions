_You may provide a variety of information about yourself to us when you register for NIMO Services; upload, purchase, view, or download certain content or products from the NIMO Services; or otherwise use the features and functionality of the NIMO Services. The information you provide to us include:

* **Account information** includes, username user ID or similar identifier, date of birth, gender.

We will neither use the results of such operations for other purposes nor upload or store them on the server. These information is not shared with any third parties.
