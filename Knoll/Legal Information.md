12. ### Cookies and Interest Based Advertising
    
    We use Cookies to receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our Site through your computer or mobile device. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access most websites on the Internet or open certain emails. Cookies allow a website to remember information about you, such as your user preferences, unique identifiers, and other information. More specifically, we use Cookies to personalize your experience when you visit our Site, uniquely identify your computer for security purposes and enable us and our third party service providers to serve ads on our behalf across the Internet.
    
    We and other third parties, engage in interest-based advertising to deliver advertisements and personalized content that we and other advertisers believe will be of interest to you. Third parties will be responsible for their own use of Personal Information collected through the Cookies.
    
    Advertisements may be delivered to you based on your online or mobile behavior (on our Site and on third party websites), your search activity, your responses to one of our advertisements or communications, the pages you visit, your general geographic location, or other information. These ads may appear on our Site or on third party websites. The third parties with whom we work to help us conduct interest-based advertising may be members of self-regulatory associations such as the [Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)](https://thenai.org/about-online-advertising/) and the [Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)](http://www.aboutads.info/). For any Site directed at persons located in the European Union, we may work with third parties who are members of the [European Digital Advertising Alliance (eDAA)](https://www.youronlinechoices.com/). Visit the links provided to exercise choice with respect to interest-based advertising or click on the AboutAds icon on a MillerKnoll advertisement and follow the instructions on how to opt-out.
    
    If you change computers, devices, or browsers; use multiple computers, devices, or browsers; or delete your Cookies, you may need to repeat this process for each computer, device, or browser. Opting-out of interest-based advertising will not opt you out of all advertising, but rather only interest-based advertising from MillerKnoll or its agents or representatives.
    
    You may also see advertisements for third parties on our Site or other websites or properties, based on your visits to, and activities on, our Site and other websites or properties.
    
    Visit each MillerKnoll brand website to view the respective Cookie List in their Cookie Notice and learn more about how to update your Cookie settings.
