**1\. Introduction**
--------------------

Hushed is founded on the principle of helping people keep their private communications private without compromise — as they should be.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to outline AffinityClick Inc.’s (“**AffinityClick**”, “**we**”, “**us**” or “**our**”) commitment towards respecting your privacy and to comply with the applicable privacy laws and associated rules governing how or when any of your personal information is collected, used and disclosed while accessing our Website and Services (each as defined in our Terms of Service located at [**https://hushed.com/privacy-policy/**](https://hushed.com/privacy-policy/). We know that you take your personal data and information very seriously; and so do we. We strongly encourage all of our users to familiarize themselves with our Privacy Policy to understand any and all circumstances where both non-identifiable or personally identifiable data may be collected or processed while using our Services.

By visiting our website, registering with or using any of our Services, you are accepting and agree to comply with this Privacy Policy.

**European Residents**: If you reside in the European Union, the European Economic Area or Switzerland, please also see our European Privacy Policy below for further information on your rights under applicable European privacy laws.

**California Residents**:  If you reside in California, please see our California Privacy Rights section below for further information on your rights under the _California Consumer Privacy Act_.

Your access to and use of the Services is subject to our Terms of Service (“**TOS**”). Any capitalized terms used but not defined in this Privacy Policy have the meanings provided in the TOS.

**3\. How We Collect Information**
----------------------------------

We collect information about you from a variety of sources when you access our Website or Services, including: (a) directly from you (when you sign up for or use our Services); (b) from our business partners and service providers; (c) from third party sources; and (d) automatically from devices that you use to connect to our Website or Services.

**4\. Types of Information We Collect**
---------------------------------------

The amount and type of information that we collect about you depends on the nature of your interaction with our Website and Services. We collect information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of your interaction with us. We collect the following information based on different scenarios of usage while accessing our Services:

**a) Registration/Profile Information**

i. when signing up for your account, we collect your email address and password; passwords are hashed and encrypted before being transmitted to our servers; we do not see the clear text version of your password;

**b) Contact Information**

i. authentication information (e.g. your account’s email address and encrypted password);

ii. communications with us (e.g. interaction with our sales or customer support teams);

iii. when buying phone numbers from certain countries, we may ask you to submit proof of identity and/or address verification information in order to comply with the requirements of local regulations in the territories from which the phone number(s) originate; we may share that information with our third party telecommunication providers in order to fulfill the transaction requirements and provide the phone number to your account; and 

iv. we allow you to add other contacts and phone numbers to your app’s address book, this information is stored until you choose to delete the contact information or your account; we do not store or download contacts from your device’s address book.

**c) Usage Information**

i. like most website and app operators, we collect information about your interactions with the Website and Services, such as which screens or pages you visit, what you click on, when you perform those actions, language preferences, and so on);

ii. message content contained within SMS/MMS segments as well as the source and destination numbers are stored until you delete your message history, associated phone number, or your account;

iii. CDR (Call Detail Records) are stored until you delete the call log, associated phone number, or your account (CDRs contains various attributes of the call such as time, duration, completion status, source number, and destination number); 

iv. voicemail recordings and voicemail greetings are stored until you delete voicemail recordings, voicemail greetings, the associated phone number, or your account; and

v. you are entirely and solely responsible for any information that you disclose or share with other users through our Services. We strongly recommend that you use extreme caution in sharing any personal information with other parties through our Services

AffinityClick is not responsible for any information that you disclose to other parties through our Services.

**d) Billing Information**

i. payment information is collected by our third party payment providers such as Apple, Google, Stripe or Coinbase, each of which may require you to provide your billing details and financial information corresponding to your selected method of payment (e.g. a credit card number and expiration date). In most cases, we will have access to transaction reference numbers and/or your billing information pertaining to each payment; however, we do not store or otherwise process any credit card information. Please refer to each payment provider’s terms and conditions and privacy policies as applicable. For ease, here is a link to Stripe’s privacy policy: [https://stripe.com/us/privacy](https://stripe.com/us/privacy) and following is a link to Coinbase’s privacy policy: [https://www.coinbase.com/legal/privacy](https://www.coinbase.com/legal/privacy)  

**e) Information from Third Parties and Integration Partners**

i. we collect your information or data from third parties if you give permission to those third parties to share your information with us or where you have made that information publicly available online (e.g. if you enable service integrations with DropBox or Slack, they may send us your information which they have collected, all of which will be controlled by such third party); and

ii. other sources (to the extent permitted by applicable law we may receive additional information about you, such as demographic data or fraud detection information and warnings, or other information about you from partner networks).

**f) Log Data**

i. we collect log data each time a device accesses our servers, log data includes details about the nature of each access including originating IP addresses, access times, device type, operating system versions, internet service providers, pages you view before and after using the Website or Services, links to third party applications, and hardware and software information.

**g) Cookies**

i. Our Website  uses “cookies” which enables us to remember you and understand and save your preferences. A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on your computer, and that your browser provides to the website each time you return. We use cookies to help us identify and track visitors to our sites, their usage of the Website, and their Website access preferences, to improve our Website and Services. If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer you should set your browsers to refuse cookies before using our websites. If you do this, the drawback is that certain features of our Website and Services may not function properly and this may also block cookies for any other website you visit; and

ii. Ads appearing on any of our Websites may be delivered by advertising partners, who may also set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by us and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
