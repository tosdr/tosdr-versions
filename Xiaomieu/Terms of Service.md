### About us

* MIUI Dev and Xiaomi Europe were founded in October of 2010 with official conscent from Xiaomi founders in Beijing to provide MIUI ROM users a community for English MIUI Android ROM and Xiaomi products. Since 2010 our web site has officially worked with Xiaomi on a variety of projects for APP translations (MIUI, Mi Home products) and bug fixing forming a large part of the Official Xiaomi Global ROM experience thanks to the great team at xiaomi.eu and its community.
