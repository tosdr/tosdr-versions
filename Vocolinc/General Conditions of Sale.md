Shipping Policy
===============

### Delivery from VOCOlinc.com

All items will be shipped within 1-3 days upon receipt of payment  (EXCEPT Saturday and Sunday, Legal Holiday ).

**We deliver to the United States and Canada only via your local delivery company.**

**Shipping Options :**

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| **US** | Standard Shipping | 2~10days | $0  |
| **Canada** | Standard Shipping | 2~10days | $0  |

**Free Shipping on ALL Orders Over 100$**

****What countries do you ship to?****

US & Canada only.  
We cannot deliver to the following areas:  
1\. Alaska  
2\. American Samoa

Thank you for your understanding.

****How will my delivery be shipped?****

Amazon functions as our logistics partner to ship out Vocolinc products. They will choose the carrier that they think works best for our customers.

****I ordered more than one item. Will they all be delivered at the same time?****

We try to make sure all your items reach you at the same time. Sometimes our products are not always sent together since different shipping options can be used, depending on the product. Once an item has been shipped, you will receive a shipment notification email.

****Can I change the delivery address of my package after it has been shipped out?****

Unfortunately, we cannot change the shipment once the product is en route.

****What do I need to do when I received a different product than what I ordered?****

Please contact customer support at [support@vocolinc.com](mailto:support@vocolinc.com).
