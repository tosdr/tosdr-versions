4\. What information do we collect about you?
---------------------------------------------

### Information you give us

You choose to give us certain information when using our services. This includes:

* **Contact details**, such as your name, surname and email address, country of residence, and a password.
    
* **Customer Support**: If you contact our customer service team, we may collect the information you give us during the interaction.
    

### Information we receive from others

* **Other Partners**: We may receive information about you, such as your contact details, from our partners who operate within the framework of surveys.
    

### Information collected when you use our service

* **Device information**: We collect information from and about the device(s) you use to access our services, including
    
    * hardware and software information such as IP address, device ID and type, device -specific settings and characteristics, browser type, version and language, operating system, time zones, identifiers associated with cookies or other technologies that may uniquely identify your device or browser;
    * information on your wireless and mobile network connection, like your service provider and signal strength.

* **To receive surveys**: we will collect your year and month of birth, your gender, your postcode, your marital status, education, employment and income range.
    
* **Cash out details**: including your PayPal ID, your PayPal address, your PayPal email, your cash out amount and date, your gift card cash out amount and date, your charity cash out amount and date.
    

We may collect some demographic data which may be special category data which is more sensitive. We will ask you to explicitly consent before we store any such data. You may withdraw your consent at any time, and we will delete any special category data that we hold. You may control these settings on your profile page or request us to do so.

If the personal information we hold about you is incorrect, please let us know.

You may decide not to provide your personal information to us. However, in that case, please note that we may not be able to fully provide you with all our services.

11\. What are your privacy rights?
----------------------------------

In certain circumstances, you may exercise the rights available to you under the applicable data protection legislation as follows:

* Right to access personal information held about you and to ask for copies of your personal information.
* Right to request us to make any changes and rectify your personal information if you think it is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask for information to be completed if you think it is incomplete.
* Right to ask for your personal information to be deleted.
* Right to restriction of processing your personal information in certain circumstances.
* Right to object to processing your personal information where the processing of your personal information is based on our legitimate interests. If we use your personal data for direct marketing, you can ask us to stop and we will comply with your request.
* Right to object to and not be subject to a decision which is based solely on automated processing (including profiling) which may produce legal effects concerning you or could significantly affect you.
* Right to data portability. You can ask us to transfer your personal information to you or to a third party you have chosen, your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine- readable format. Please note that this right only applies in certain circumstances.
* Right of withdrawal consent at any time with effect for the future. In each of our email communications, we provide you with a corresponding link to manage your preferences or unsubscribe. You can also contact us by email.
* Right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory. The supervisory authority in the UK is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) who may be contacted at https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/, however, we would appreciate the opportunity to handle your concerns before you approach the ICO, so we ask that you contact us first in this instance.

To exercise any of your rights set out above, please contact us at [support@qmee.com](mailto:support@qmee.com).

We respond to all requests we receive from users in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We may ask you to provide proof of identity before we can answer the above requests. In some cases, we may reject requests for certain reasons (for example, if the request is unlawful or if it may infringe on trade secrets or intellectual property or the privacy of another user).
