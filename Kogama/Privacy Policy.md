1\. General
-----------

This Privacy Policy governs the collection of personally identifiable information on all platforms on which KoGaMa ('The Service') provides its services, i.e. the kogama.com and kogama.com.br websites, any website(s) on which our games are embedded, or in connection with the use of The Service's mobile apps available on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

We aim to collect only the minimum necessary data to operate the site, and likewise to keep data storage duration to a minimum. This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and made a part of the terms and conditions of The Service. Please refer to the Data Collection section below for information on what personal data we process, and how we store this data.

By registering for an account, accessing or using The Service, users agree to be bound by the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

### 1.1 GDPR: The short version

* We store your IP adress in order to facilitate technical operation of the site, as well as to be facilitate safety for our users
* We store your email address if you provide us with it and you are old enough to do so. We may ask for your parents email address if you tell us that you are less than 13 of age in order to inform that an account has been created and ask for consent for certain features
* We use ads to keep the service operational. If you’re under 13, we do not share any personal data as a result of this, but otherwise we request your consent to share certain data with our partners to facilitate a personalized ad service (See below for more).

### 1.2 Cookies

The Service uses cookies. A cookie is a small amount of data (a small file) sent to your computer or device by a website. In general, when you visit The Service, cookies will be placed on your device that help us provide you with a good browsing experience and also allow us to improve its website and display adds. More specifically, The Service uses cookies for the following purposes:

* Web Analytics. We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics places cookies on your device to help distinguish between different visitors and to analyze how users use The Service. The cookies placed by Google on your device are used to collect certain information, such as the date and time that a user visits us, the number of times a user has visited us and The Service that has directed the user to us. More information on the use of cookies by Google Analytics can be found on Google’s website.
* Functional Cookies. We also place cookies on your device to make navigating The Service easier. These cookies are used to allow the website to remember choices you made and provide for enhanced, more personal features. For instance, these cookies can be used to remember the status of your login, or to allow you to return to a previous page.
* Social Media. Social media can place cookies through The Service to integrate social media (i.e. LinkedIn, Twitter, Google+ and Facebook). You agree to place these cookies on your device by clicking on the social media buttons to activate them. These social media have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we do not control. Please check the social media websites for more information about their cookies and how to manage them.
* Ads. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google’s use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users of The Service based on their visit to The Service and/or other sites on the Internet. You can visit aboutads.info to opt out of third party interest based ad cookies.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. Please note that if you do so, The Service will not function as intended.

3\. Data collection
-------------------

We receive and store certain types of information whenever users visit the site. In addition to the information individuals provide explicitly on The Service (An anonymous username, voluntary e-mail address ), we may collect the name of the domain and host from which users access the Internet; the IP address of the computer used; the browser and operating system used; the date and time of access; and the Internet address from which you arrived at The Service (including any search terms used to find The Service through a search engine such as Google).

### 3.1 Your KoGaMa account

To use all functionalities on The Service you must create a KoGaMa account. Upon creation of a KoGaMa account, we ask you to choose a username - please be sure to pick one that doesn’t include any personally identifiable information. We also ask you to identify your month and year of birth in order to identify extra needs for consent. You will also be asked to provide an e-mail address if you’re above the age of consent, or an email address of your parents or guardian if you are not. This is entirely voluntary, and if you choose to do so it will only be used to communicate news from the games on The Service.

You may play games on The Service without creating an account, but only if you are 13 years or older.

### 3.2 User-to-user communication

The Service allows users to communicate in a number of ways. Private chat and in-game messages are only stored long enough to ensure stable delivery and then deleted. Note: revealing personally identifiable information is against the Terms and conditions, and it should not occur. Any personally identifiable information revealed in online activities will be available to others who can access this content. We will delete personally identifiable information, if we are made aware of a breach of this rule.

### 3.3 GDPR consent

Upon accessing The Service, you will be presented with a dialogue that allows you to either consent to us processing personal data as outlined in this Privacy Policy, or to decline to the processing of personal data. If you consent, you may choose to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at support@kogama.com.

If you withdraw your consent, we will cease processing of the data for which you have withdrawn consent, unless we have a legal obligation to keep some or parts of your data. The withdrawal of your consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on your consent before its withdrawal.
