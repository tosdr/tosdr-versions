### What information do we collect?

* * *

We collect information from you when you register an account on our web site, submit information using our contact form, or make a purchase from our online store.

When making purchases, you may be asked to enter additional details for the purpose of payment processing. Any other information that is required to process a payment will only be retained on our system until the sale has been completed. You may, however, browse our web site without registering an account.
