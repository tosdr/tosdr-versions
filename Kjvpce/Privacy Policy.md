About Us
========

![1611 first edition of the Authorized Version of the Bible by Cornelis Boel](//kjvpce.com/images2019/bible_about.jpg)

[Authorized King James Version Bible](#card-element-115016)

Our website features the Authorized King James Version of the Holy Bible which is God’s providentially appointed Word for the whole world which reveals the name of the Father as “Jehovah,” and the name of His Son, “Jesus,” in Whom there is salvation, and the name of the “Holy Ghost,” Who indwells every born-again Christian believer.

1. Works like a Bible APP (only requires an internet connection).
2. 100% AD-FREE.
3. Easy to use website navigation.
4. Intelligent bookmark system.
5. Customization (The settings menu has many useful features for users).
6. Mobile Friendly (built using the latest Bootstrap 4 Front-end framework).
7. Easy to share with other people using the share icon
8. Multi-language support. Read the NT in Greek. Read the Bible in English or Simplified \\ Traditional Chinese.
9. Font support for dyslexic readers (OpenDyslexic).
10. SSL Secure Connection.

[Dyslexia-Friendly Bible](#card-element-317000)

Our King James Bible website now supports optimal fonts and colors to improve readability for people with dyslexia. To activate this feature, please follow the instructions below:

1. Go to the [settings menu](https://kjvpce.com/settings).
2. Select OpenDyslexic under "Font Family".
3. Click the green "Update" button near the bottom of the screen.

A dyslexia-friendly Bible is also available on [Apple Books](https://books.apple.com/us/book/id1531270176) and [Nook](https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/kjv-dyslexia-friendly-bible-derek-ziemer/1137367546?ean=2940162899402) for FREE.

[Text to Speech](#card-element-159543)

Text to speech works on later versions of Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox using browser extensions. I recommend [Natural Reader](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/natural-reader-text-to-sp/kohfgcgbkjodfcfkcackpagifgbcmimk?hl=en) or [Read Aloud](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/read-aloud-a-text-to-spee/hdhinadidafjejdhmfkjgnolgimiaplp?hl=en) for Chrome. Read Aloud also works on Firefox and can be downloaded [here](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/read-aloud/). Supports English and Chinese.

[Navigation](#card-element-117000)

These notes are designed to introduce you to our navigation system:

![Website screenshot on navigation system](https://kjvpce.com/images2019/website.png)

1. **Tweet**: Click this icon to share the current page on social media (Twitter). The message can be customized to your liking.
2. **Breadcrumb navigation**: Navigate to any book of the Bible with just a few clicks. Switch to Old Testament \\ New Testament.
3. **Search Bar**: Here you can put in the Book, Chapter and \\ or verses that you want to search for. Book abbreviations are acceptable e.g. (Gen, Lev, Exo) Chapter is required, verses are optional. See the above screenshot for an example.
4. **Settings menu**: Click this icon to goto the settings menu. Change font family, font size, font color and many other settings in this menu. In this screenshot we are using the Nunito font family, 20px font size, burnt umber as the font color and highlighting the Words of Jesus in bold style.
5. **Bookmark page**: Intelligent bookmark system. Click the bookmark icon to bookmark any page on our website (where available). To return to that bookmark, click the (homepage) button; bookmark information will now appear on the top (if using a mobile phone) or top-left corner (if using a desktop \\ laptop) of the homepage.
6. **Copy Verses**: Click the Copy Verses button to copy all verses to clipboard. Easily share these verses on social media, with your mobile phone contacts or to your desktop using (CTRL-V).
7. **Chapter navigation:** Click the previous and next buttons to go **back or forward** 1 chapter.

[Bible Reading Tips](#card-element-690142)

Follow these _Bible reading tips_ to make the most out of your visit to our website.

1. If you haven't already, customize your Bible reading experience through our [settings menu](https://kjvpce.com/settings). This is the most unique feature of our website that sets us apart from other websites.
2. Pray and ask God to be filled with the Holy Spirit before reading the Bible. The Holy Spirit guides us to all truth. - [John 16:13](https://kjvpce.com/bible/john/16/13)
3. Be consistent. Try to read the Bible every day so that you can be filled with the power of God's word.
4. To help you be consistent, start one of our three [Bible reading plans](https://kjvpce.com/reading-plans). If you've never read through the Bible before, start with the [New Testament](https://kjvpce.com/bible-reading-plan/new-testament/day/1) reading plan as it can be completed in just 54 days.
5. For reading plans, enable the 'estimate reading time' feature in the [settings menu](https://kjvpce.com/settings).
